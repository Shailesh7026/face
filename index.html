Guidelines

Here are the guidelines for using this feature:

  • Remove hats, sunglasses, or any face-covering accessories.
  • Maintain a neutral facial expression; avoid extreme emotions.
  • Avoid using filters or effects.
  • Upload high-resolution photos; avoid blurry images.
  • Ensure your head and shoulders are within the frame.
  • Keep hair away from your face.
  • Look directly at the camera.
  • Avoid shadows on your face.
  • Use a recent photo that accurately represents your current appearance.
  • Only one person should be in the photo.
  • Hold the camera steady or use a tripod to prevent blurriness.
  • Ensure your entire face is clearly visible.
